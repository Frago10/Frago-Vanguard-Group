8 ─── LOCALE (EN ⇄ ES) ──────────────────────────────

Floating EN/ES toggle bottom-right (always visible).

Engine: data-i18n / data-i18n-html / data-i18n-aria attributes drive
dictionary lookup in TRANSLATIONS object inside main.js. Persists to
localStorage["fvg-locale"].

Hero title re-runs char-split on locale change (innerHTML replaced →
splitTextRoot rebuilds chars → .is-in added → cinematic re-entry plays).

Brand vocabulary (Impacto, Confianza, etc.) stays in Spanish in both
locales — it's the brand, not a translation.

Translations are voice-coherent, NOT literal:
  - "compound" verb → "componerse"
  - "patient capital" → "capital paciente"
  - "operators" → "operadores"
  - "long horizon" → "horizonte largo"
