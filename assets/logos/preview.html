UX verdict

For an institution that wants to feel timeless, credible and quiet — the three strongest candidates are UNIFIED (01), MARQUE (02), and HORIZON (06).

UNIFIED is the safest evolution — recognizable continuity with what you have today, but resolved into one silhouette. MARQUE is the most heritage-leaning and reads as 100-year institutional. HORIZON carries the firm's actual thesis in the geometry itself — the line below the V is the long horizon.

MONOLINE (03) is best for foil-stamp / engraved applications. FOIL (04) is the most "design-forward" without losing class. SEAL (05) is the most archival — closer to a banking institution's coat-of-arms.
